# Verification policy

Verify every packet completion criterion and referenced Must requirement with reproducible evidence. Record outcomes as `PASS`, `FAIL`, `BLOCKED`, or `NOT_RUN`; only `PASS` satisfies coverage. Required checks may include static analysis, unit, integration, contract, migration, security, performance, accessibility, recovery, and rollback tests when specified by the approved plan.

Recommend pass only when all required checks pass, no accepted blocking review finding remains, evidence is reproducible, and deviations are zero or explicitly accepted by the human.
