# Verification policy

Verify every packet acceptance criterion and mapped Must requirement with reproducible evidence. Record outcomes as `PASS`, `FAIL`, `BLOCKED`, or `NOT_RUN`; only `PASS` satisfies coverage. Required checks may include static analysis, unit, integration, contract, migration, security, performance, accessibility, recovery, and rollback tests when mapped by the approved plan.

Recommend pass only when all required checks pass, no accepted blocking review finding remains, evidence is reproducible, and deviations are zero or explicitly accepted by the human.
